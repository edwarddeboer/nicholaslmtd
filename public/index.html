FAQ

Do I need to know anything about crypto before working with you?

Nope! I work with total beginners and seasoned investors alike.

Is my money safe?

I provide education and strategies that prioritize your financial safety and long-term success. I don’t directly hold client assets.

How much experience do I need to start investing in cryptocurrency?

No experience is necessary! I work with clients at all levels, from those who are new to crypto to those with more advanced knowledge. My goal is to guide you through the process at your own pace.

Can you help me with traditional investment strategies as well?

Yes! While my focus is on cryptocurrency and digital assets, I also provide guidance on traditional investment strategies, such as stocks, bonds, and mutual funds, to ensure your portfolio is well-rounded and aligned with your long-term goals.

How often will we communicate about my investments?

Communication frequency depends on your preferences and needs. I offer regular updates, quarterly reviews, and can schedule one-on-one meetings as often as needed to ensure you're comfortable and informed about your investment strategy.
